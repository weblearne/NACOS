About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science at Audu Bako College of Agriculture, Dambatta was established to provide students with quality education in computing, information technology, and related fields. Our department is committed to preparing students for the challenges of the digital era through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

With state-of-the-art computing facilities and experienced faculty members, we offer programs that meet industry standards and equip our students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in their careers. Our curriculum is regularly updated to reflect the latest developments in the field of computer science.

We aim to produce graduates who can contribute effectively to Nigeria's digital transformation and compete favorably in the global job market. Our focus areas include programming, database management, networking, software development, and computer applications in agriculture.

Our Programs

National Diploma in Computer Science

A two-year program designed to provide students with foundational knowledge and skills in computer science and information technology.

Duration: 2 years NBTE Accredited

Higher National Diploma in Software and Web Development

An advanced two-year program for ND graduates who wish to deepen their knowledge and skills in specialized areas of Web Development.

Duration: 2 years NBTE Accredited

Higher National Diploma in Networking And Cloud Computing

An advanced two-year program for ND graduates who wish to deepen their knowledge and skills in specialized areas of Networking And Cloud Computing.

Duration: 2 years NBTE Accredited

Research Areas

Agricultural Informatics

Application of computing technologies to improve agricultural productivity, farm management, and agricultural extension services.

Database Systems

Design, implementation, and management of database systems for efficient data storage, retrieval, and analysis.

Artificial Intelligence

Development of AI algorithms and applications with a focus on agricultural systems and natural resource management.

Computer Networks

Research on network protocols, security, and IoT applications in agricultural and rural settings.

Software Engineering

Development of methodologies and tools for creating efficient, reliable, and user-friendly software systems.

Mobile Computing

Research on mobile applications and services that can support agricultural activities and rural development.
